How do you usually celebrate your birthday?
In England birthday parties are a lot of fun! All the children like this holiday. They bring presents and cards for the Birthday Girl or Boy. Everybody wears party hats and nice clothes.
There are always lots of tasty dishes, drinks and a birthday cake. You can see colourful balloons in the room. Children play different games and have fun. They are happy!
After that the main character blows out the candles on the birthday cake and makes a wish. Then everyone sings «Happy Birthday».
Birthday is a great holiday!
